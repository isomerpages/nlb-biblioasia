**About**:	We bring together literary enthusiasts from Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, mainland China, and Taiwan, creating a vibrant cross-cultural community that celebrates the diversity and unity of Chinese-language literature across different regions.

*Mission*<br> 
To provide an open and welcoming platform where friends who share a passion for reading

*Vision*<br> 
To establish ourselves as a cherished haven for all who love reading and sharing

*Goals* <br>
Through thoughtful reading and authentic sharing, we aim to enhance collective wisdom, enrich members' spiritual lives

Our members participate in discussions with a relaxed and joyful spirit, naturally sharing their reading reflections and personal experiences in an environment that encourages openness and genuine dialogue.
